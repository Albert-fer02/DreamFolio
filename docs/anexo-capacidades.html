03 Engineering Approach
I
Integrity over convenience
Data systems must assume adversarial conditions. Correctness is non-negotiable; shortcuts compound into failure.
II
Performance as a primitive
Latency is a design constraint, not an optimization. It must be considered from the first architectural decision.
III
Composable autonomy
Systems should operate independently and converge via robust, well-defined interfaces — not implicit coupling.
